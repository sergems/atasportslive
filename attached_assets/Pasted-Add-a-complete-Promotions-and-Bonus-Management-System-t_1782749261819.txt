OVERVIEW

ATA wants to reward users who deposit funds.

Whenever an administrator activates a promotion, users who deposit money through Pesapal (or any supported payment gateway) automatically receive bonus credit.

This bonus is NOT cash.

It is promotional credit.

The bonus credit:

✓ Can ONLY pay for live streaming access.

✓ Cannot be withdrawn.

✓ Cannot be transferred.

✓ Cannot be used for betting.

✓ Cannot be converted into cash.

Implement a "Bonus-Eligible Livestream" flag in the admin panel. This allows administrators to choose whether a particular livestream can be paid for using bonus funds. Premium events (such as championship finals or exclusive PPV matches) can be marked as Bonus Not Accepted, requiring users to pay from their cash wallet instead. This gives ATA greater flexibility in managing promotional campaigns and maximizing revenue while still rewarding users.
